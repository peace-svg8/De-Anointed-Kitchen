Who We Are

More Than Just Food,
It's an Experience

Welcome to De Anointed Kitchen (DAK), where passion for authentic Nigerian cuisine meets exceptional culinary service. Located in the heart of Abraka, Delta State, we have built a reputation for delivering meals that don't just satisfy hunger, but create lasting memories.

Founded by a passionate chef with a deep love for Delta's rich culinary heritage, our journey began with a simple belief: "Our Food Dey Speak for Us." We don't just cook; we craft every dish with carefully selected fresh ingredients, rich spices, and the authentic techniques that make Nigerian food world-renowned.

Whether it's our signature smoky Jollof rice, a rich bowl of Egusi soup, or perfectly baked cakes for your celebrations, we put our heart into every order. We cater to individuals, intimate gatherings, and large-scale events, ensuring every bite reflects our standard of excellence.
